We offer weekly family American Sign Language classes. By targeting the acquisition of ASL, families are able to share traditions, family history, and juicy family gossip! Center for Chronic Illness (Seattle, WA Virtual Support groups)

Living with chronic illness brings many challenges, which can leave you feeling overwhelmed and isolated. Our support groups are designed to provide a safe space to meet others impacted by ongoing health challenges. Licensed mental health professionals facilitate all of our groups. These professionals offer guidance and structure, helping you get the most out of your group meetings. Currently has 16 different active virtual support groups based on different chronic illnesses varying on different days of the month/week.
